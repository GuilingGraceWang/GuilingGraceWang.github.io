Dear YWCC Students,

Thank you for your interest in research. Please note that I will NOT respond to email inquiries. I encourage you to review the following information and discuss it with your academic advisors:

  1. Explore Faculty Research: Please visit faculty webpages to learn about their research projects. You can contact faculty members directly to inquire about opportunities to work on projects that align with your interests.
  2. Research Meeting: Midway through each semester, I organize a research meeting where professors offering paid projects, MS thesis/projects, and undergraduate research opportunities present their work. You are encouraged to attend this meeting and connect with professors. An academic advisor will also be present to address any logistical questions. The meeting typically takes place on a Wednesday during the common hour from 2:30 to 4:00 PM. Please monitor your email for announcements regarding this event. Again, I will not respond to individual emails.
  3. Additional Resources: Please visit this page for further information. This page will be updated following each research meeting.

Thank you for your attention to these guidelines.
Prof. Wang
